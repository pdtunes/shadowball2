How Does It Work

With its flattened edge, the ShadowBall rugby skills development training aid has 5 panels and is engineered to rebound and spin back when the flat edge is passed or thrown against a wall.

High quality, durable, five panel, hand stitched and made to official size and weight specifications, the ShadowBall enables players of all ages and levels to perfect passing, catching and throwing skills and techniques – in their own time, unsupervised, at their own performance pace, and independently of a training partner.
